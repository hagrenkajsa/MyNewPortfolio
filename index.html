My Projects

Truck Security

#UX | #UI DESIGN | #USER RESEARCH

When concluding my studies, I completed my master’s thesis in collaboration with Volvo Group at their Experience Design team. The focus of my research was to develop a tangible concept for a holistic security system that enhances security for truck drivers in relation to cargo and fuel theft on the road. The project followed the Design Thinking methodology in order to understand user needs and from that, design an add-on service that creates value for all stakeholders, including the driver, fleet owners and Volvo Group.

About Me

My Academic Background

Master of Science Program in Interaction Technology and Design - Umeå University, Aug 2020 - Jun 2025

The program combines computer science and interaction design, where we have developed the skills to create both the interface and the underlying technology behind applications, websites, systems and other digital services, with a strong focus on the users’ needs. Design thinking and agile work flow are commonly used in our projects to create an outstanding user experience.

South Korea, Exchange semester - Chung-Ang University, Aug 2024 - Jan 2025

During my studies, I went on an exchange semester in South Korea, where I combined my major with exploring Korean food, culture and nature. I met many interesting people from all over the world and have learned a lot about them and their cultures, something I believe will be useful in my future work.

My Strength / Competence

UX & UI Design

One of my strongest skills and passions lies in UX design, where I find it fascinating to research user behavior, perception and problems and to explore how to meet their needs with solutions that are both aesthetic and functional. I strive to create experiences that go beyond expectations. I have a keen eye for shape, color, and content hierarchy, and I always keep the user’s perspective at the forefront of my design process.

My greatest strength is my creativity. I love exploring new approaches to design, blending innovative ideas with established design principles and always a strong focus on accessibility.

Front-End Development

In addition to UX design, I have a strong interest in front-end development. Most of my experience is in implementing mobile applications using React Native with JavaScript, as well as developing web applications with React. My solid CSS skills allow me to experiment with colors, shapes and UI structure, which is my favorite part of the front-end development!

Through my education, I’ve also gained foundational knowledge in back-end development. While back-end isn't my primary focus, this background gives me valuable insight into what's possible when designing for users, providing a fuller perspective that I find beneficial when working on various projects.
